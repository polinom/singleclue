Rules
Game Setup
Split into 2, 3 or 4 teams.
Assign one Word Master to each team. Other are Agents.
Open Game Board on a tablet or phone and place it in the middle, visible to all.
Larger screen is better, as all players will be clicking on words to reveal colors.
If you are a Word Master, open the Chief Card on your phone. Sync the Chief Card with current game by entering the 4-digit game ID found on Game Board.
Make sure Agents can't see this card!
Game Play
First team starts the Round. Word Master looks at the Chief Card and tries to come up with a single word clue to hint words that belong to his/her team.
Word Master states a single word, plus number indicating how many cards the word relates to. Careful not to hint to words belonging to other teams! For example, you can use the word "Fruit" to combine "Apple" and "Kiwi".
Agents discuss what they think their Word Master meant. Then, after making a collective decision, one of the agents clicks the words on the Game Board. Ideally, agents should open only the words of their own color. As soon as they make a wrong guess, the turn ends and the next team starts guessing.
The round ends when all Word Masters had a chance to hint words to their Agents. First team to have all its words revealed at the end of a round wins the game. You may have more than one winner.
When giving clues, Word Master can't say a word containing the same root as any word on the Game Board.
